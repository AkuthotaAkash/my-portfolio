Soil Contamination

This mini project aims to raise awareness about soil contamination and its harmful effects on the environment and human health. It explains how harmful chemicals from industries, waste, and pesticides pollute the soil, making it unsafe for farming and living. The project highlights the need to protect soil through proper waste management, reducing chemical use, and promoting organic farming. By sharing simple solutions and encouraging eco-friendly habits, the project helps people understand their role in keeping the soil clean. It supports a healthier environment and promotes sustainable practices for the well-being of current and future generations.

Traffic Sigh Detection and Voice Alert Using Neural Networks

The Traffic Sign Detection and Voice Alert Using Neural Networks** mini project helps in recognizing road signs and giving voice alerts to drivers. It uses a camera to capture traffic signs and a neural network model to identify them correctly. When a sign is detected, the system gives a voice warning, helping drivers stay alert and follow traffic rules. This project improves road safety by reducing accidents caused by missed signs. It is especially helpful for new drivers or in poor visibility. Overall, it makes driving safer and smarter by using technology to understand and respond to traffic signs in real time.
